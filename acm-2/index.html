The acmbot
Smart Hoops System

Innovative Smart solutions that improve the working lives of Basketball Players everywhere.

LOWER ENERGY CONSUMPTION. LONGER RUNTIMES.

Energy efficiency is at the heart of acmbot. While other robots clean for up to 5 hours, our R3 Scrub and Leobots are designed to deliver up to 10 hours—twice as long on a single charge.

LESS WATER USED. BECAUSE EVERY DROP COUNTS.

Effective design means our R3 Scrub uses 85% less water (6L/hr) compared to traditional scrubbers (40L/hr).

BUILT TO LAST.

Every part is optimally designed and engineered with durable materials. With a lifespan of up to 7 years, our acmbot outlast the average 4-year lifespan of other robots.

Reduce the carbon footprint

Lots of CO2 comes from cleaners taking transport to the workplace, however with our Robots you do not need to transport daily, thus lowering the carbon footprint!
